About Event

PLASMA is annual flagship event of Dept of ECE. It is National level tech fest where students from all across the state and country visit and compete with hundreds of enthusiasts in an array of Robotic and Non-robotic events! In the 3rd Edition of PLASMA, we had 800+ teams among which 150 were from other states and the remaining were from various districts of Karnataka. In the subsequent year, PLASMA was hosted virtually which witnessed tremendous participation from all over the state and nearby regions. The current iteration of PLASMA 2022 boasts a winning amount of up to 1.5 Lakh rupees and a plethora of robotic and non-robotic event.

About College

JNNCE was established in the year 1980 by the National Education Society.
The college is affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), Belagavi, and is recognized by AICTE, New Delhi.
It offers 8 UG and 2 M.Tech Programs besides MBA and MCA.
It has 9 Research centers recognized by VTU. Spread over 39.4 acres, the campus has the amenities to cater to the needs of over 3400 students and 400 employees .
Each department is equipped with a good infrastructure with qualified and experienced faculty and well-equipped laboratories.
The Institution has 46 doctorates and 56 research scholars to its credit.
The college is regarded as one of the excellent centers for technical education and research.
The college hosts a number of international and National workshops Conferences, seminars, and short-term courses throughout the academic year.

About Department

The Department of Electronics & Communication Engineering was started in the year 1980.
Department has well-equipped laboratories, qualified and experienced staff to cater to the needs of students of the UG program.
The ECE department has been accredited by NBA, New Delhi and recognized as a research center
under which 22 research scholars are pursuing Ph.D. under VTU Belgaum. UG students are actively involved in carrying out their projects at
New Age Incubation Network and NewGen IEDC. The department manages Institute of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers (IETE),
Shivamogga center and has IETE student forum. IETE is a national apex body of professional engineers devoted to advancements in Electronics,
Telecommunications, IT, and allied areas. The 56th center of IETE was inaugurated at
Jawaharlal Nehru National College of Engineering Shivamogga on 1st March 2009
